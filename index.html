Port Activity — Seasonal Comparison

Daily port calls and chokepoint transits: current year vs historical average. 7-day rolling average.

Port calls and chokepoint transit calls (the number of vessels arriving at a given port or passing through a critical maritime passage) are tracked daily using satellite data and serve as a timely indicator of potential supply chain disruptions. The chart compares current activity against the historical average, with the shaded band representing normal variation. Sustained drops below this range may signal emerging trade disruptions warranting attention.
